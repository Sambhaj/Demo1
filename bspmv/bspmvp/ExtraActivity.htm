Innovation
Innovation for Tribal Awareness is Innovative Programme conducted in the college for the last five years. This programme includes several issues like Malnuitrition, Superstition, Health, Alcoholism, Small Savings, Environmental Awareness and Family Planning. It has brought unique result in Manbhang, bhilkheda and adjoining villages in Chikhaldara tahsil.
