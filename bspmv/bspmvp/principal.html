More About our College
Education plays an important role in the overall development of the nation. Higher Education is all the more important in shaping the destiny and standing of the country at the international level. Higher education should provide necessary skills and knowledge that prepare and allow all individuals to become productive members of a knowledge-based society.
Education Institute is known by the courses it runs, the teaching faculty, and the office staff. Ours is one of the oldest institutions in the town catering to the educational needs since 1969. We are proud of the fact that most of the students of our college are from adjoining tribal villages of Melghat and the nearby villages in the vicinity of Achalpur and Chandur Bazar tahsils.Through Tribal Cell we try our best to inspire and motivate our students and increase their participation in various activities.
As Principal of Bhagawantrao Shivaji Patil Mahavidyalaya, Paratwada its my foremost responsibility to provide physical facilities for smooth conduct of academic, sports and other activities in the college. We have a very pleasing sight and enough space and infrastructure to accommodate these activities. We have spacious lecture-halls, laboratories for Functional English and Home-Economics, and well built and equipped Computer lab.
Along with Arts and Commerce at Undergraduate level we have Post-graduation in Marathi and Home-Economics and Post graduation in Commerce. Recently, we have started English medium commerce at Undergraduate level.
Observance of strict discipline, dress-code, providing identity cards in the campus is the integrated part of our institution. Physical fitness is given prominence through participation in indoor and outdoor games at college, university and inter-university level. Medical examination and Physical fitness Tests are conducted every year.


Vision
"A committment to guide our students to achieve excellence through value education."

Goals and Mission

1 To impart qualitative and valuable service in the field of education to the residents of Paratwada and nearby rural and tribal areas of Melghat.
2 To attain community and social development through infrastructure facilities of the institution.
3 To ensure and inculcate perfect discipline in terms of regularity, sicerity and punctuality amongst the students so that they contribute to the society and nation as most respectable and responsible citizens.
4 To aim at overall personality development of the students fraternity through extra-curricular activities in association with various social and cultural organizations.
5 To provide a platform to the students by giving them an opportunity to face all the challenges of the competitive world, with utmost utilization of their potential in sports, athletics and other events.


Objectives

In working towards realizing our vision and mission, we at Bhagawantrao Shivaji Patil Mahavidyalaya have set for ourselves the following objectives of education.
1 Impart quality education by providing an environment conducive to teaching and learning.
2 Improve students' performance at examination.
3 Recognize and encourage excellence in academics, cultural and sports activities.
4 Encourage and assist research activities.
5 Cultivate leadership qualities.
6 Provide opportunities for team building, setting and achieving targets.
7 Connect the world of academics with the world of work.
8 Inculcate a sense of discipline, social responsibility and community service.
9 Imbibe secular principles and respect for eachother's faith and ideologies.
10 Transform our students in to competent and confident, sensitive young men and women.
