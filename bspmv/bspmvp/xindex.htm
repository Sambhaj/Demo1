Introduction
     Bhagawantrao Shivaji Patil Mahavidyalaya, Paratwada is committed to offer qualitative and value based education that does not compromise on academic scholarship. We provide opportunities to you to participate fully in your personal and intellectual journey. Our mission is to create environment for the students to attain knowledge and skills to face the challenges of the globalised world.
We have well-qualified and experienced faculty, committed to, and who endeavour to keep in tune with the latest developments in the respective subjects, and keenly interested in parting knowledge to the students. We want you to excel in academics, but academics must not be your only area of achievement. We would like you to participate whole-heartedly in sports, extra-curricular activities, and activities to attain overall development of personality.
We encourage our students to let their minds soar to lofty heights and dreams, with faith and trust in God, courage and determination.
It is our sincere hope that every student of our college grows spiritually, intellectually and socially. This alone creates our distinctiveness, one that we both cherish and desire to continue forever.
