Admission Policy

    1. Admissions to our college are based on the rules and regulations of the government and SGB Amravati University, Amravati.


   2. All admissions are valid only for one Academic session/Semester and are to be renewed by Application in the prescribed form for every subsequent year/Semester of study in college.


   3. A student may not be allowed to continue for the next term/semester of the Academic year on account of unsatisfactory discipline/attendance/academic record.


   4. No admission shall be regarded as duly granted unless it is granted by the authority of the principal.
