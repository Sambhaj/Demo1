B.S.Patil college library was started in 1969. Books are organized subject wise according to the Dewey Decimal Classification System into three major categories.

  1. Reference Section
  2. Textbook Section
  3. Subject Books
  4. Miscellaneous

Information Services
Library offers various services to its users. They include:

• OPAC online public access catalogue services is available
• Last 5 years old question bank is available
• Special collection of Competitive-Exam books
• Deposit scheme for outsider students & teacher
• Reader club for teacher & citizen
• New arrival services is available
• Inter library loan facility is available
• SMS services to faculty
• UGC-INFLIBNET N-LIST facility

Membership

B.S..Patil Library offers membership to Professors, and Students enrolled in the current Academic year. Ex Students / Others can avail the reference facility by permission from Principal / Librarian.

Timings

The library remains open from 8.00a.m to 5.00p.m.on all working days except Sunday and Public Holidays.
