Activities
 Sports

Along with mental fitness there is enough scope for physical fitness through various games and sports. The college has enough space for sports activities and preference is given to both indoor and outdoor games. Our students excel in games and sports. The tribal students of our college have always enriched the events and matches at local and University level. Our students have secured colour coats and won laurels for the college. Students of both Junior and Degree college have distinguished themselves at the local, district and State level.
NSS
The NSS unit of our college comprise of 200 volunteers. The regular activities and the special camp aim at all round development of the personality of the students. Special camps in tribal villages pave way for catering information and knowledge among rural and tribal masses. Our NSS volunteers have participated in university, state and national level camps. The clothes stitched under practical work in Home -Economics lab are distributed among tribal children and elders. We try our best to create civic sense among people and our students. Incentives are regularly given to NSS volunteers and sports persons.
