Internal Quality Assurance Cell

Introduction

       In presence of the National Action Plan of the NAAC for performance evaluation, assessment and accreditation and quality enhancement of institution of higher education, the NAAC proposes that every accreditated institute establish an Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) as a post-accreditation quality sustenance measure.

Goals

As highlighted in the guidelines, the goals of IQAC are –
1. To develop a quality system for conscious, consistent and catalytic programmed action to improve the academic and administrative performance of HEIs.
2. To promote measures for institutional functionality towards quality enhancement through internationalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.

Functions

1. Development and Application of quality benchmarks for various academic and administrative activities of an institution.
2. Dissemination of information on various quality parameters of higher education.
3. Organization of workshops, seminars on quality related themes.
4. Documentation of various programmes.
5. Preparation of AQAR and to submit it to NAAC.
