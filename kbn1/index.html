Introduction

      Friends, Khadayata Business Network Forum was formed in 2010. The thought behind it was that, if other communities like Jains, Marwaris & Kutchis can do it why can't we Khadayatas.

      In September we had our first impromptu meet & we christened the Group as KBNF - Khadayata Business Network Forum. KBNF is for Samasta khadayatas Ekdas. KBNF had a few successful meets at Khadayata Bhuvan (Vile Parle) where we met every month. KBNF had a family get together & a picnic to Kanheri caves.
