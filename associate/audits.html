Audits and Assurance

In a dynamic economic environment, Assurance is an imperative tool for all stakeholders of an organization including its management and shareholders as well as the Revenue Authorities. It is the most commonly used yardstick to measure the effectiveness of controls and corporate governance in an organization.

Over the past 5 decades, we have worked with a suite of clients, ranging from small owner-managed businesses, partnership and charities to listed companies, multinationals and government bodies.

Predominantly, our Audit and Assurance services cover the following :

❖ Statutory Audits under the Companies Act 2013
❖ Tax Audits under the Income Tax 1961
❖ Audits under the Maharashtra Value Added Tax Act (MVAT) 2002
❖ Compilation of financial statements
❖ International accounting standards
❖ Governance and Risk Services
❖ IT audit
❖ Counter Fraud reviews
❖ Reviews and Compilations
